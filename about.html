About Me

My name is Saimon Karki. I am a student studying in Grade 11 at Kavya School. I live in Gokarneshwor and have a strong interest in graphics design.

I have worked on various creative projects and even contributed designs for a clothing brand named Zillion. This experience helped me understand real-world design needs and improve my skills in visual communication.

I enjoy creating posters, logos, and social media designs. My goal is to become a professional designer and continue improving my creativity and technical skills.

I also completed training from a teaching institute named Broadway Infosys, where I received professional certification.
